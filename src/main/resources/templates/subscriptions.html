Find valid ids for stop places and quays at stoppested.entur.org. Only stops based on national ids will be handled by ukur (ie. starting with "NSR:").
New subscriptions will receive push messages after these rules:

  • ET messages: If a subscription has one 'From stop' and one 'To stop' in correct order in an EstimatedVehicleJourney with delays, the entire EstimatedVehicleJourney is pushed after recorded calls and other estimated calls are removed to minimize the message size.
  • SX messages: If one of the stops from a subscriptions is affected by a PtSituationElement, the entire PtSituationElement is pushed after all affects are removed (this causes important information to be lost, and will be fixed in ukur)
